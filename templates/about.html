About STEM Pathfinder ℹ️

Built for NJ TSA 2026 to help students in grades 7–12 explore STEM careers with honest, transparent guidance.

Our Purpose 🎯

STEM Pathfinder helps middle and high school students explore STEM careers in a way that's honest, encouraging, and actionable. We show you exactly what each career requires — from the courses to take in ninth grade to the certifications that unlock your first job.

No account. No tracking. No ads. Your data lives only in your browser's session and disappears when you close the tab.

How Recommendations Work 🔍

The scoring is transparent and deterministic — it works the same way every time:

Raw Score = (1.0 × interest tag matches)
             + (1.5 × strength-derived tag matches)
             + (0.5 × work-style matches)

Final Score = normalize Raw Score to 0–100
Ties = broken by # of matching technical skills
  • Interest matching: Each career has topic tags. We count how many overlap with what you selected.
  • Strength bonus: Strengths like "analytical thinking" map to interest tags like "math" and "data," weighted 1.5× because they signal a natural fit.
  • Work style: How you like to work (collaborative, hands-on, etc.) adds a small bonus when it matches a career's style.
  • No black boxes: The score is entirely based on the tags you see on each card. Simple and honest.

Privacy 🔒

  • No account or login required.
  • Your quiz answers are stored only in your browser's session cookie — never sent to a database.
  • No tracking, analytics, or advertising of any kind.
  • Closing your tab clears all saved data.

A note for students & families 💙

STEM Pathfinder is a starting point, not a final answer. Career paths are deeply personal and shaped by many factors beyond any quiz. Please discuss your interests and plans with a school counselor, trusted teacher, or family member before making big decisions. This tool exists to open doors — not close them. You've got this! 🚀
